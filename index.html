About Us

A trusted name in WA construction

Priority Construction is a leading provider of integrated fabrication, welding, mining construction, shutdown services and project-delivery solutions in Australia. With extensive expertise and strategic locations across the country, we cater to the needs of clients in a wide range of industries.

Founded in 2021, we started our journey as a small crew supplying boilermaker and welding services in Western Australia. Today Priority Construction fields over 30 personnel and services companies such as BHP, Rio Tinto, FMG and Water Corporation.
